Bed Bug Services

 

 

Eliminating bed bugs is a very difficult and detailed process that requires cooperation from all parties involved. At Vital we perform a rigorous inspection of the room or rooms to determine the severity and scope of the infestation. We then formulate a plan utilizing IPM standards, monitoring and the best chemicals known to eliminate Bed Bugs. We provide a complete list of items and procedures that must be followed before and after service. We guarantee our work for a full 60 days, that means if a bed bug shows itself within those 60 days we will come out at no charge. These protocols have been tried and tested over many years and when properly followed elimination is GAURENTEED.
